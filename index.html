I like puzzles

In early 2003 my friend Brendan Furneaux visited me, and reawakened my interest in burr puzzles.

We wrote some code together to generate complex burr puzzles, then we got a tablesaw and router from the Berkeley Tool-Lending Library and made them out of wood. Getting the precision right was real hard.

It was a great visit.

This puzzle was our favorite.

It has many multi-piece moves, a 77 move solution, and it takes 17 moves to remove the first piece.
